Our Charity Work

Here at Cake First Girls, charity means a lot to us!

That's why we donate 10p from every cupcake to fund Girls Education, and you'll regularly see our cupcakes at the various charity events we sponsor.

We are able to offer our cupcakes at a discounted price, for you to give away or re-sell at your nominated event. To get started, we will need confirmation from your chosen charity that you are raising on their behalf and details concerning your event. Orders must be placed two days in advance. If you're ready, please submit your details via the contact form on our 'Help' page, explaining a bit about yourself and the charity you are raising for.
